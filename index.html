About Toyland

Toyland is a nation in the border between Asia and Oceania. The nation is made of 6 states, 2 territories, and 3 autonomous regions. The country is full of rich culture, friendly people, and amazing sights. Toyland gained independence on August 10, 1781 from Kuishi Niku Cheka. Learn more information and learn how to travel by going to the travel page.

Six of the seven stripes on the flag represent the six states of Toyland. The last stripe represents all the other teritories in the nation. The green diagonal stripe represents the fertile land, with the big blue representing peace and bravery. The yellow star represents our union.

Immigration to Toyland

Immigrating to Toyland is easy. You will need to work in Toyland for at least 2 years in order to apply for a green card. After about 3-5 years, you will be approved citizenship if qualified.

If you would like to petition another family member, they will get a temporary resident visa immediatley after all the paperwork. It will take about 2-3 years to get a green card, and 3-5 years to get citizenship.
