Toy Central State

About Toy Central State

Toy Central State is home to the capital of Toyland, Toy Central City. It has a population of over 20 million people, making up over 20% of the entire Toyland population. The state is known for its beautiful works of art on the streets, friendly people, amazing rural scenery, and having an economy larger than Colombia. We hope you would like to visit us, as we are warm and welcome to visitors and tourists.

The Toy Central State flag has a huge inspiration from the Toyland national flag. The green diagonal line and the blue triangle all mean the same as the Toyland flag, but the three stripes symbolising us being the third state to join the union against Kuishi Niku Cheka. The star in the middle is to represent that we are the nation's capital, and that we are the geographical center of the Toyland main island.
