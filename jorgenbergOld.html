Jorgenberg

About jorgenberg

Jorgenberg is a self-declared independent dictator ruled nation off the coast of Kuishi Niku Cheka. Although they are self-declared, Toyland recognises them as a part of Kuishi Niku Cheka. Jorgenberg broke off after the victory against Jorgen Wurtz, making Jorgen Wurtz II the first dictator of the "nation." The people in the island are under heavy propoganda, and are not exposed to anything.

The flag of Jorgenberg was the old Toyland flag. After Toyland won and adopted a new flag, Jorgenberg kept the same flag. The blue and white stripes symbolise peace and purity, the red symbolising bravery, the green border symbolising our fertile land, and the white star symbolising a nation.
