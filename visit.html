VISIT TOYLAND

Get a Visa

Go to your nearest embassy's website and contact them that you are going to apply for a visitor's visa. A visitor's visa costs about $50. It takes about 1-2 weeks to get your visa after being approved. A visa lasts for 30 days, and you can extend it by paying $15 every extra 14 days.

Research destinations

Research the best destinations to be in Toyland. My personal recomendations are Toy Central City, Legoland City and its Giant Chess Sculpture, Jorgenberg and the Jorgen Wurtz Museum, Small Town and the Small Town Museum, and much more! Feel free to explore around the country!

Book a flight

Now that you know your vacation plan, book a flight! There are tons of connecting flights and straight flights to all around the world powered by our national airline, Happy Day Airlines. Other airlines also give connecting flights to Toyland. You will most likely land in Toy Central City International Airport

Explore

Now that you have landed safely in Toyland, EXPLORE! We are a warm and welcoming country, and we would love you touring the country. Whether it be a small tour around Toy Central City, or a nationwide tour, it is our honor to guide you in your vacation. Have fun travelling!
